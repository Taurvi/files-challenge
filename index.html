Alexei Volkoff (Алексей Волков)

Alexei Volkoff (Russian: Алексей Волков) is the head of Volkoff Industries, a front for an international arms smuggling ring.

Volkoff was born in Moscow, Russia and is fluent in Russian, English, and German. Volkoff has one daughter. There is no information on the identity of his wife, however it is presumed he is divorced.

Volkoff has many connections, particularly with the Russian Mafia. These connections have helped Volkoff quickly rise to international prominence for arms trafficking. Volkoff posesses a unique skillset which suggests former employment with the Committee for State Security (KGB). It not known whether or not Volkoff has ties to the KGB's successor, the Foreign Intelligence Service (SVR).

Volkoff is currently wanted in the United States for several charges, including conspiracy to murder and to destroy property of the United States government. Volkoff is considered to be heavily armed and extremely dangerous. He is to be deatined immediately upon sighting.
