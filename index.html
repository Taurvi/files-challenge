Alexei Volkoff (Алексей Волков)

Alexei Volkoff (Russian: Алексей Волков) is the head of Volkoff Industries (Russian: Волков Промышленности), a front for an international arms smuggling ring. Volkoff was said to have been born in Moscow, Russia and is fluent in English, Russian, and German. Little is known about his life execpt that he has many connections with the Russian government and the mafia. It is believed that because of his connections, he previously worked with the KGB and may be a collaborator with its successor, the SVR. Volkoff is currently wanted in the United States for several charges, including conspiracy to murder and to destroy property of the United States government. There is very little information about Volkoff and his operations. Attempts have been made to infiltrate his organization by several agencies, however agents either turn up dead or are currently missing.
